IT Service Management, where do I start? New

I remember asking this question to my manager, and that was over a decade ago. This is an important question, especially when you are starting out a career in the Information Technology or even looking to grow your career in the industry.

First, you are at the right place and while I cannot answer this question for you, I can help you in selecting your answer.

Have you tried our free IT Service Management? If not, try it now (https://itsmtrainingacademy.com/survey.html) After you have completed the survey and still mulling over your options and next step, we hope that below steps will assist you in selecting your next steps:

  1. Familiarize yourself with IT Service Management, and ITIL. You can visit AXELOS website (https://www.axelos.com/); this organization has vast information that can really give you a good overview and an insight into IT Service Management
  2. You can reach to IT Service Management professionals in LinkedIn and are welcome to join free professional IT Service Management. Here you will learn about what does the role entail, what your day to day in the world of Service Management may look like.
  3. Register for one of our upcoming free informational webinars
  4. Just do it. You have taken the survey and have also followed through Step 1 to Step 3, and are still mulling over, we strongly recommend that you take ‘Just do it’ attitude and register for one of our upcoming class.
