ITIL® 4 Foundation

  • Start your career in IT Service Management
  • ITIL® 4 Foundation Training is accredited by PeopleCert on behalf of AXELOS
  • Our ITIL® 4 Foundation course includes official course materials and sample exam papers
This ITIL® 4 Foundation course includes the overview of ITIL® framework and its role in modern ITSM. The course covers the following topics:

Introduction

  • What is IT Service Management?
  • Key components of ITIL® V4
  • ITIL® V4 Four Dimension model, Practices and Processes

Overview of following topics:

  • Key Concepts of Service Management
  • The Four Dimensions of Service Management
  • The ITIL® Guiding Principles
  • The Service Value Chain
  • General Management Practices
  • Service Management Practices
  • Technical Management Practices
This ITIL® 4 Foundation courses is suitable for following professionals:

  • Looking to start a career in IT Service Management
  • Looking to grow your career within IT
  • Software Engineers
  • Business Analysts
  • Project or Program Managers
  • Functional Manager
  • Test Analyst/Lead/Manager
  • HR Managers overseeing IT Resources
This course includes:

  • The ITIL® 4 Foundation Examination voucher
  • Sample papers and Mock exam
  • Official course material
  • Certificate
  • Training provided by an experienced and an accredited trainer
  • Email support
The ITIL® 4 Foundation exam tests knowledge of the basic terminology, concepts, and principles behind ITIL® and service management.

  • Type: Multiple Choice
  • Duration: 60 Minutes
  • Open Book: No
  • Total Questions: 40
  • Pass Mark: 26/40
